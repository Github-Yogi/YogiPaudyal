About

With migrating to the country at a young age, came the struggles of fitting in. I often found solace in technology and computers, I'd find myself staying up countless nights watching LinusTechTips videos, marveling at the capabilities of computer hardware. From the hum of the hard drive to the flow of the code - I was encapsulated by its beauty.

My love for both hardware and software brought a difficult decision. Which path would I take? Turns out I could do both which brings us to now, in the final year of my Computer Systems Engineering degree at Brunel University London. Presently like most, I am deeply intrigued by the field of AI and machine learning as I continually seek out ways to enhance my workflow through its endless applications.

Where my career path will take me is a mystery so I walk one step at a time. My current plan is to complete my Master's degree abroad: closer to family and somewhere I can explore my various other interests during my downtime. Owning my own business in the software industry is my north star as I hope the leave the world 1% better than I found it.
